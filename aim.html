The activities covered at Cricket Academy include

1.Skill development sessions through drills and net practice.
2.Offer the latest technical developments via biomechanical analysis.
3.Practice matches held periodically to develop game sense and tactical skills.
4.Physical fitness with conditioning sessions.
5.Provide development programmes for individuals, groups and teams.
6.Mental skill development.
7.Teaching Laws of the Game.
8.Motivational talks by professionals.
9.Injury prevention.
10.Conducts coaching certification programmes for coaches in Canada.
11.Umpiring seminars.
12.Conduct NCCP Workshops.
